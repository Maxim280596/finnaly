How isometric video creates? How we create videos?

How it works when you order an animated video? There are six
stages of production: scriptwriting, styleframe, storyboard,
animation, voiceover application and sound design.

Order now
Scriptwriting

A well-performing video should have a well-thought script. Script is the text that will be narrated by a voiceover artist. To keep the viewer’s attention for a long time is a challenging task, that is why we should do our best to shape a clear message and transfer it creatively.  Our specialists can create an engaging script for your video. However, you can write it on your own, and we can optimize it for the best results.

Styleframe

The script is ready and our illustrators start working. They create style that emphasizes the individuality and uniqueness of the project and agree the style with you. It is very useful to get the style examples you like so that designers could focus on them.

Storyboard

After the style is approved by you our illustrators draw storyboard (the whole package of illustrations for the future video). While they are working on it, we get the voiceover.

Voiceover

Voiceover is recorded by a professional voiceover artist with a studio equipment. Our expert voice over artists have years of training and we’re happy to provide them with specific guidelines for your unique script.

Animation

Now it’s time for the much-awaited stage. This is where the animated explainer video starts to take shape! Here, our animators provide the illusion of life to the created visual style doing custom animations and effects.

Custom copyright music & SFX

At the last stage our designers make all the needed revisions and improvements and our sound designer starts creating audio effects to make your video even more immersive and memorable. The impressive audio helps maximize the audience’s interest.

Final release and happy client!

After all we release a final video to you. You can review the video at each stage of production and after release too. We use convenient services for you to leave comments on storyboard and draft video such as Boords and Frame IO. You are able to request revisions for 3 times at each stage of production and we are open to grasp
your vision and produce the video.
